    ENGINE STAFF

    The engine staff are the people working in the steam engine which pulls the carriages. They maintain the main train engine itself. Fuelling the furnace with coal, Keeping the pressure stable so it produces steam, Managing the brakes, Keeping tabs on the water levels, Driving the train. Requirements: Understanding of the steam engine to be able to maintain it Engineering qualification

    TRAIN STAFF

    Train staff is a job related to the train, however this varies from checking tickets of the passengers to maintaining the train carriages and keeping them clean and tidy for journeys. They also help load and unload passengers' luggage onto and off the train and as well as helping the passengers safely get on and off the train. Lastly, they have to signal the engine staff to depart when all the luggage, passengers and staff have safely boarded the train. This job requires attention to detail, focus and physical strength needed to help load and unload luggage as well as being able to communicate with and help passengers. Fluency in English would be recommended for this role as it involves communication with passengers and other members of staff as well as a friendly and positive attitude.
